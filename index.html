Our Story

A neighborhood institution.

Tucked just off the Lehigh University campus on W. 4th Street, The Goosemen has been a gathering spot for students, faculty, and South Side locals for decades.

Owner Tony runs the counter with the same warmth and care that's kept people coming back since the eighties. Meat is cut fresh to order, hoagies are piled high, and the specials are written on paper plates — because some things shouldn't change.
